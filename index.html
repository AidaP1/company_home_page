Our Tables

All of the tables at The Dragon's Ease are custome-made for 4 - 12 players. They feature felt tops, rounded and padded corners, as well as a host of other attachments for your dice, cards or drinks. We'll assign you a table based on your party size, but let us know beforehand if you need a little extra space to conquer your foes.
